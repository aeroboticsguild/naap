About the Guild

We are the FIRST-ever proposed robotics organization in the entire history of the National Aviation Academy of the Philippines since 1969!

Vision

To become a premier student organization that develops future-ready innovators, problem-solvers, and technology-driven leaders in robotics, automation, and applied engineering.

Mission

Cultivate students' interest and competence in robotics, electronics, programming, automation, and innovation through projects, training, competitions, and community engagement.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is the membership fee?

The membership fee is ₱50.00, which includes your official RFID-enabled Guild Identification Pin.

What tracks can I join?

Members can join one of four tracks: Systems & Fabrication, Software & Programming, Research & Development, or the Multidisciplinary Track.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! The Guild welcomes members of all skill levels. You'll start with a skills assessment and be placed in the appropriate track to develop your abilities.

How do I advance in rank?

You advance by earning points through attendance, project participation, competitions, and leadership roles. Higher ranks unlock more opportunities and recognition.

Can I join other organizations?

Yes, regular members can join other organizations. However, Executive Board members cannot hold positions in other organizations to ensure full commitment to the Guild.

When is the membership deadline?

The membership drive is open until July 31, 2026. Apply now to secure your spot!
