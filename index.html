About the Guild

We are a duly recognized student organization of the National Aviation Academy of the Philippines — Palmayo-Basa Campus.

Vision

To become a premier student organization that develops future-ready innovators, problem-solvers, and technology-driven leaders in robotics, automation, and applied engineering.

Mission

Cultivate students' interest and competence in robotics, electronics, programming, automation, and innovation through projects, training, competitions, and community engagement.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Innovation
  • Collaboration
  • Excellence
  • Responsibility

Why Join? Practical Benefits

Beyond passion, the Guild gives you tangible, career-shaping advantages that set you apart.

Skill Validation

Official ranking from Foundation to Expert, with certificates for every training you complete.

Tool & Lab Access

Use expensive equipment, components, and workshop spaces without personal cost.

Built-in Project Teams

Find verified coders, fabricators, and researchers for thesis and competitions.

Thesis Foundation

Competition Pathway

Represent NAAP in regional, national, and international robotics & innovation events.

Industry Linkages

Access internships, sponsorships, and partnerships through the External Relations desk.
